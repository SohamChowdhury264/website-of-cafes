Nestled right opposite Pioneer Park's main gate in Barasat, The Forest Brew is more than just a cafe — it's a living, breathing green sanctuary where every corner tells a story.

From our lush plant-draped walls to our tranquil pondside seating, we've crafted a space where Gen-Z creators, students, couples, and dreamers can slow down, sip something beautiful, and simply be.
